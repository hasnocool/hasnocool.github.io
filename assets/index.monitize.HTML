Register for War Thunder Camouflage Installer

Registration is simple and gives you access to basic features. Here's how it works:

  1. 1. Download and install the War Thunder Camouflage Installer app.
  2. 2. Launch the app and click on the "Register" button.
  3. 3. Enter your email address and choose a password.
  4. 4. Your registration will be logged, and you'll have access to basic features.

Once registered, you can use the app with basic functionality immediately.

Access Levels and Premium Features

Our system offers different levels of access:

  • Basic Access (Free): Available to all registered users
  • Premium Access: Unlock advanced features with a one-time payment
  • Founder's Access: Full feature access for early supporters (limited time offer)

How it works:

  • Your registration date is logged in our system
  • When you make a payment, we'll send you a unique access key
  • Enter this key in the app to unlock the corresponding premium features
  • The app checks your access level with each launch, ensuring you always have access to the features you've paid for

This system ensures that your access is tied to your account, not your device. You can use the app on any computer, and your premium features will be available as long as you're logged in.
